Our Workshops

Software Development

Software Development is a field of study that focuses on designing, building, and maintaining computer programs and applications. At CPEC SAINT BABETH TSS, students are trained in both theoretical concepts and practical skills required to create modern digital solutions.

Learners gain knowledge in:

Programming languages such as JavaScript and Python
Web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
Application design and problem-solving
Database basics and system development

Through hands-on practice in the software development workshop, students learn how to build websites, applications, and real-world projects that prepare them for employment or further studies in ICT.


Learn coding, web development and app creation. “Our Software Development workshop equips students with practical programming skills, enabling them to design, build, and maintain modern applications that solve real-world problems.”

Multimedia Production

Multimedia Production involves creating digital content using different forms of media such as images, audio, video, and animation. This program equips students with creative and technical skills to produce engaging visual and digital materials.

Students learn:

Graphic design and image editing
Video production and editing
Photography and visual storytelling
Animation and digital media tools

With access to a multimedia workshop, students gain practical experience in producing professional content for media, advertising, education, and entertainment industries.



Video editing, animation, photography skills.“Our Multimedia workshop empowers learners with creative and technical skills in graphic design, video editing, and digital content production for today’s media-driven world.”

About Us

CPEC SAINT BABETH TSS is a private school
in Gicumbi District offering both general
education and technical training. The school provides
Ordinary Level studies and specialized programs in
Software Development and Multimedia Production (L3–L5).
With modern workshops and qualified teachers,
the institution focuses on practical skills,
innovation, and preparing students for the
job market. The school promotes discipline,
creativity, and excellence, aiming to become a leading
center of technical education in Rwanda.
